## Design Context

### Users
- **Dual-audience**:
  - **End users** signing in / verifying identity (often non-technical, task-focused).
  - **Developers** integrating OAuth (technical, precision-oriented), primarily via the **Developer Portal** and **OAuth consent** flow.

### Brand Personality
- **Secure, professional, trustworthy**.
- Emotional goal: **confidence + control** (clear states, predictable outcomes, no surprises that feel risky).

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Sleek tech**: crisp typography, high-contrast surfaces, restrained accents, “security-grade” clarity.
- Theme: **both light and dark** (with a toggle when/if implemented).

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity beats cleverness**: every screen should be scannable in under 3 seconds.
2. **Delight without doubt**: micro-interactions should reassure, never distract or obscure security-critical actions.
3. **Accessible by default**: strong focus states, reduced-motion support, readable contrast.
4. **Consistency = trust**: shared components/tokens across login, MFA, consent, and portal.
5. **Developer-grade precision**: validation, errors, and empty states should be specific and actionable.
